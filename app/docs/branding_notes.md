# Branding Notes

These notes outline the design direction for the final OverRead identity.

## Visual Theme
- **Concepts**: A magnifying glass over a document, a speech bubble with an overlay frame, or a glowing transparent box highlighting text.
- **Color Palette**: Modern Deep Blue or Purple. High contrast. Simple, clean vectors.
- **Constraints**: 
  - Do not use images of real people, real copyrighted manga/webtoons, or trademarked characters in the branding or screenshots to avoid Play Console strikes.
  - Do not create an icon that closely resembles Google Translate or other official Google tools.
  - Keep the app label strictly as `OverRead`.

## Assets Needed Configured Before Launch
- `ic_launcher` (Foreground and Background for Adaptive icons).
- `512x512` Play Store Icon.
- `1024x500` Feature Graphic.
- Clean vector files for floating buttons and internal icons.
