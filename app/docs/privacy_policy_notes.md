## App Purpose
OverRead is a utility application that provides on-device screen translation by overlaying translated text directly on top of screen content. 

## Permissions Explanation
- **Display over other apps**: OverRead uses this to show a floating translation button and to display the translated text right over your comics or apps.
- **Screen Recording (MediaProjection)**: We use this to capture what is currently on your screen so we can find the text to translate. We only capture a single frame when you explicitly tap the translate button. There is no continuous screen recording.
- **Internet Access**: OverRead only uses the internet to download the official Google ML Kit language models needed to translate text offline.

## Local Processing
All Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and translation happen strictly on your device. OverRead does not upload your screenshots, detected text, or translated text. We do not have backend servers storing your data.

## Internet & Model Downloads
To perform on-device translation, OverRead needs to download language models from Google's servers. This is the only time the app uses the internet for its core features. No user behavior or screen content is transmitted during this process.

## What is Not Collected
- We do not collect analytics.
- We do not collect account or profile data.
- We do not collect location, contacts, files, or microphone data.
- We do not store screenshots.
- We do not store a persistent history of translated text or apps you translated over.

## Data Retention
Because OverRead processes all capture frames strictly in memory and immediately discards them after rendering the text, there is no personal data retained. You can clear your local preferences and downloaded language models simply by clearing the app's storage in your Android device settings or by uninstalling the app.

## User Controls
You have full control over when OverRead reads your screen. It only activates when you press the floating button. You can permanently stop the app and all of its background processes at any time by pressing "Stop Floating Button" in the app or the Quick Menu.
