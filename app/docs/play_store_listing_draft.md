# Play Store Listing Draft

## Short Description
Translate text on your screen with a private floating overlay.

## Full Description
OverRead is a lightweight, on-device translation tool that helps you read comics, webtoons, manga, and foreign apps seamlessly. Whenever you see text you want to translate, simply tap the floating button. OverRead will instantly translate the text and overlay it nicely right where you found it, preserving the original reading experience.

**Key Features:**
- **Instant Overlay Translation**: Translations appear directly over the original text.
- **One-Tap Capture**: Effortlessly translate with a single tap of the floating button. There is no continuous recording to drain your battery or bypass your privacy.
- **100% On-Device Processing**: Your privacy is our priority. OverRead processes screenshots and translates text locally on your device using Machine Learning (ML). No data is sent to the cloud.
- **Designed for Readers**: Optimized for webtoons and comics where you cannot simply copy and paste the text.

**Privacy-Focused Design**
We do not collect or store your data. OverRead captures your screen *only* when you explicitly tap the translation button. The screenshot is analyzed locally and immediately discarded. Internet is used strictly once to download the official Google ML Kit language models needed for translating. OverRead does not upload or save any screenshots, detected text, or translations.

**Required Permissions:**
- **Display over other apps**: Essential to show the floating button and the translated boxes above your screen.
- **Screen Recording (MediaProjection)**: Needed to capture the screen when you tap the button so we can read the text. The prompt is presented natively by Android system.
